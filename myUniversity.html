"Start Here, Go Anywhere"

Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC) is located at 245 Greenwich Street, New York, NY 10007. It is one of the many CUNY Schools here in New York. It offers various majors such as: Computer Science, Nursing, and early child hood education.

BMCC has many clubs and activities that you can join. Some of the clubs are: art club, programming club, photography club.
